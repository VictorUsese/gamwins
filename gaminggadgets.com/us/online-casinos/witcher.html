1. Lack of payment options for deposits and withdrawals
2. No licensing and regulation
3. No customer support 
4. Poor games selection – a limited games selection could be a sign of a lower-quality casino.
5. Lack of reputable software providers – established software providers only partner with trustworthy casinos.
6. Bad external reviews – look out for blacklists, warning signs, and negative reviews from other players.
7. Absence of popular games – well-known games are popular for a reason, and their absence could be a warning sign.
8. Slow to payout winnings – a casino that takes too long to pay out winnings could be a sign of a potential problem.
9. Too good to be true bonuses – excessively high bonuses or promotions that seem too good to be true could be a warning sign of a potential scam.
